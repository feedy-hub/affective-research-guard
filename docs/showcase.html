Research Dialogue Guard Skill

先稳住人，
再打磨问题。

Protect the state.
Sharpen the idea.

面向科研讨论、论文构思、实验设计和技术路线推演的展示页。它强调在复杂对话中先识别混乱、过载与压力，再用严格科研标准审视 idea、证据与下一步。

A showcase page for a research-dialogue Skill that first notices confusion or overload, then reviews ideas, evidence, experiments, and next steps with rigorous academic standards.

1. 状态检测
讨论是否已经混乱、过载或过度悲观？
2. 科研审查
问题、贡献、证据、baseline 和风险是否清楚？
3. 可执行下一步
转化为最小验证实验或明确写作修改。
1. State Check
Is the conversation overloaded, unclear, or overly pessimistic?
2. Research Review
Are the question, contribution, evidence, baselines, and risks clear?
3. Next Step
Convert the discussion into a minimal test or concrete revision.

Rigorous Review

从严格导师和审稿人视角检查 idea、方法和实验。

Reviews ideas, methods, and experiments from a strict advisor/reviewer lens.

最小验证路径

Minimal Validation

优先找到最能证明核心命题的验证路径。

Identifies the shortest path to test the core claim.

核心理念：支持研究者的状态，但不替代证据；鼓励继续探索，但先把问题压实。
Core idea: support the researcher without replacing evidence; encourage exploration while keeping the problem testable.
